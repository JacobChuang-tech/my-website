"The Book of Elon": If others work fifty hours a week and you work a hundred, you'll accomplish twice as much in a year. If others work forty hours a week and you work a hundred, what takes them a year, you'll achieve in four months.

Couldn't agree more. I'm currently 31 with about four years of work experience, but the hours I actually put in every week far exceed 50. The experience and growth I've gained are well beyond my peers. If you want to accelerate your growth, just do more than everyone else. I'm glad that even before I realized I could accelerate my growth by investing more time, I had already started doing exactly that.
